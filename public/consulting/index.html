Consulting Services

AHoosh helps companies, startups, and decision-makers use AI, research, and data to understand problems, evaluate options, and build practical solutions.

What We Do

Eight Service Areas

01 · AI Strategy & Adoption

Understand where AI actually helps

Helping companies understand where AI can actually help, what tools to use, and what to avoid. No hype — just a practical map of what applies to your situation and what the realistic outcomes are.

02 · Market Research & Opportunity Analysis

Research before you commit

Researching markets, competitors, customers, pricing, demand, and entry opportunities. Turning public data and AI-assisted research into clear answers about whether an opportunity is real and how to approach it.

03 · Business Model & Strategy

Clarity on positioning and direction

Helping founders and companies clarify positioning, revenue models, offers, and growth paths. Useful when you are starting something new, pivoting, or trying to figure out why growth has stalled.

04 · Operations Improvement

Fix what slows you down

Finding manual, repetitive, or unclear workflows and redesigning them with better tools and processes. The goal is always to free up time and reduce errors — not to add complexity.

05 · Data & Decision Support

Turn data into decisions

Building dashboards, reports, data structures, and decision-support models from available information. Helps teams stop guessing and start making decisions from real signals.

06 · Digital Presence & Website Strategy

A website that works for you

Improving websites, content structure, user flows, credibility, and conversion logic. Design, build, and run on modern stacks — built to perform and maintainable without constant developer dependency.

07 · Content & Thought Leadership

Build authority with real content

Creating research-based articles, explainers, reports, and educational content. Not generic blog posts — actual analysis that positions you or your company as a credible source in your field.

08 · Automation & Tooling

Practical automation, not vendor lock-in

Designing practical automations using AI tools, no-code/low-code systems, APIs, and existing software. Built to solve specific problems without adding fragile systems that break when someone leaves.

The Process

How It Works

1
First Conversation

We talk about what you are trying to solve. No forms, no templates — just a direct conversation to understand the situation.

2
Diagnosis

I map what is actually happening — using research, data, and direct observation — and identify where the right intervention will have the most impact.

3
Build & Deliver

We produce the output together — a research report, a strategy document, a working tool, or a live system. Scope is defined before work starts.

4
Handoff or Ongoing

You get everything documented so your team can continue independently — or we stay involved for ongoing support. Your choice.
