Being findable when customers ask AI

FAQ

Common questions

Will my business definitely show up in ChatGPT after this?
No, and be wary of anyone who promises that. The kit makes your business much easier for AI engines to find and quote, and gives you the test prompts to measure the change yourself.
I'm not technical. Can I actually do this?
If you can edit the text on your website and paste a snippet — or hand it to whoever manages your site — you can use the kit. Every template shows a completed example next to the blank one.
How long until I see anything?
Setup takes a weekend. Engines pick up changes on their own schedule, usually days to a few weeks. The honest signal is your trend over two to three months, which is what the tracking sheet is for.
Is this the same as SEO?
It overlaps. SEO chases ranking positions; this chases being named in AI answers. The changes — clearer pages, question headings, schema — tend to help your normal Google results too.
Which AI tools does it cover?
ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews are the test targets. The method applies to Claude and others too — they reward the same things.
What if the advice goes out of date?
That's why updates are included. The kit is refreshed quarterly and every past buyer gets the new edition by email, free. Each edition is date-stamped.
What if I buy it and it's not for me?
14 days, full refund, no questions. Reply to your purchase email.
